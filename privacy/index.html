Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

NexDeck ("we", "our") develops plugins for Elgato Stream Deck. This policy describes how our plugins handle user data.

2. Data Collection

Our plugins operate locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

When you use our plugins to connect to third-party APIs (e.g. GitHub, Spotify), your data is sent directly from your device to those services. We do not intercept, log, or store this data. Please review the privacy policies of those services.

4. OAuth Authentication

Some plugins may use OAuth to authenticate with third-party services. OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device using Stream Deck's encrypted storage. We do not have access to your tokens.

5. Analytics

We do not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry in our plugins.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy: nexdeck.plugins@gmail.com
